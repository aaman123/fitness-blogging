An Inspiring weight-loss Journey


by Elleah Morgan,

  • "Only I Can Change My Life, No One Can Do It For Me. ."






    • My whole life I had been overweight, with every year aged I would gain a stone (when I was 8, I was 8 stone, 9, 9 stone, 10, 10 stone, etc). Before I knew it I had ballooned to almost 21 stone.

    Throughout my life I had names thrown at me daily, I was bullied severely for my weight, on occasions I was even hit for it. I was so unhappy in myself and at one point I even lost the hope to go on. Although I was unhappy in my body, I knew I was always going to hate it and learned to accept it. I had given up hope.

    A few years later I packed up my bags and ventured to Newquay, when I arrived there I was amazed, people skating/ surfing, walking, making the most of the beautiful scenery. I found myself exploring the area and found the weight dropping off, this gave me the motivation to start dieting and eating better (1200 calories per day on a low carb diet).

    In 3 months I had managed to lose 2 stone and with that, I decided I would push myself further and take on swimming, something I'm not very experienced at as I figured this would also be a great life skill. 3 months later and 2 more stone lighter I decided to join the gym! I embraced the active lifestyle Newquay had to offer and found myself trying things I had never tried before.

    Nearly 2 years on and I am now over 8 and a half stone (122 lbs) lighter. I have found I have so much more confidence and I am very proud of what I've achieved. Instead of getting horrible remarks now, I get praise and compliments. I never thought I would achieve this, my life has only just begun and I never truly realized what life had to offer.

    My goal is to lose another 1.5 stone (21lbs) so I have lost half of my body weight.
