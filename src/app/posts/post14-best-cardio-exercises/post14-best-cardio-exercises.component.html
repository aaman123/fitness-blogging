6 Best Cardio Exercises

When it comes to fat loss the best thing that comes to your rescue is aerobic cardio exercises. Cardio is the best way to burn that stubborn body fat that has been lingering onto your sexy body for quite some time.

But worry no more let's break the best 6 cardio exercises that you can practice anywhere and anytime to keep you set in the health game.

Cardio is short for cardiovascular exercises that increase your heart rate and helps you burn fat easily. Cardio is efficient not only for optimal muscle health but also for your heart and especially for people suffering from heart diseases and diabetes. Cardio also helps in lowering blood pressure, strengthens immunity, aids in sleep, and increases the overall mental and physical health of your body.

Here are the best cardio exercises rated top to bottom that burns the most calories in less amount of time and are considered the safest while practicing.

    • Rope Skipping




      • Rope skipping is considered one of the best cardio exercises and burns the most fat in a short amount of time, only a 15-minute session of rope skipping can burn almost 1000 calories. Basic requirements for rope skipping. Jumping ropes strengthens your lower body, as well as the upper body, and exerts stress on your knees, ankles, and thighs.

    • Burpees




      • The Burpee is a full-body movement where you start in a standing position with shoulders and arms extended downwards. Flex your hip and move into a squat position with your hands on the ground. Shift your weights on the hands and fall back to the normal standing position with a slight jump of the ground.

      Burpees if executed correctly can help to develop strength and can serve as an aerobic exercise to shed fat and build muscle, as it involves a lot of explosiveness and strength it is categorized under both cardio and resistance training.

    • Mountain Climbers




      • Mountain climber is a compound movement that engages all the muscles from your neck to your glutes and helps to build strength and agility in your upper body particularly the core. Mountain climbers help strengthen your core as it is a looping movement where your core gets engaged every time your tuck in your knees. Mountain climbers do not put more stress on your lower back and spine and are considered as low impact cardio exercise.

    • Jumping Jacks




      • The true form of cardio is where you elevate your heart rate to burn the excess fat and jumping jacks are just the right exercises to do that. Try performing more repetitions in one go. Make sure you are breathing right when you are performing the exercise. Jumping jacks is an easy exercise that can be performed by anyone regardless of age and strength as it just requires you to jump without tripping, lol.

    • Running




      • And now the most popular form of cardio that everybody does voluntarily and involuntarily, running. Running is the easiest form of cardio that is prevalent all over the world as it helps in building endurance and aids in cardiovascular health. Studies have shown that running for just 15-30 minutes can kickstart your metabolism and aid in some serious fat loss.

    • Jumping Squats




      • Squats are the best and the most functional exercise that can help you shed that excess body fat and help increase overall strength and agility of your body. When a bodyweight squat is accompanied by a jump it qualifies as HIIT(High-Intensity Interval Training) workout and helps boost your cardiovascular health.
